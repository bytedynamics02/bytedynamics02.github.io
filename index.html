Event Location

Perdana Botanical Garden, Kuala Lumpur

📍 Address: Jalan Kebun Bunga, Tasik Perdana, 55100 Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

🚌 How to get there:

  • By car: Parking is available at the park (limited space, arrive early). Follow signs to “Taman Botani Perdana”.
  • By public transport: Take the KTM Komuter to Kuala Lumpur station or MRT to Muzium Negara. The park is a 10–15 min walk or short Grab ride from there.
  • By taxi/e-hailing: Set “Perdana Botanical Garden” as your destination in Grab/Taxi apps for direct drop-off at the entrance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in this run?

The run is open to everyone residing in Malaysia – citizens or non-citizens – as long as you are 3 years old and above​:contentReference[oaicite:38]{index=38}. (Children should be accompanied by an adult.) Despite “Ladies’ Wellness” in the name, men and boys are welcome to join in support of the cause.

Is the run timed or competitive?

No, this is a fun run. There will be no official timing or race rankings​:contentReference[oaicite:39]{index=39}. It’s all about enjoying the run and finishing together for a good cause. Go at your own pace – run, jog, or walk!

What do I get with my registration?

Every participant gets a race kit which includes the event t-shirt, a finisher medal, a goodie bag with refreshments, and a bib​:contentReference[oaicite:40]{index=40}. You also get access to the free health screenings and all on-site activities on event day.

How do I register and pay for the run?

You can register online by clicking the “Register Now” button on this page, which takes you to our Ticket2U registration form. Fill in your details and select your category and t-shirt size. Payment can be made via the methods provided on Ticket2U (online banking, credit card, etc.). All registrations are final – tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable​:contentReference[oaicite:41]{index=41}.

When and where do I collect my race kit (t-shirt, bib, etc.)?

Race kit collection details will be announced closer to the event on our official Facebook and Instagram. Tentatively, race packs will be available for pick-up a week before the run (for example, 1–2 days in late May) at a designated location in KL (e.g., a hospital or community center). There will be no race kit collection on event day, so please plan to collect beforehand. If you cannot make it, you may send a representative with a copy of your IC/passport and confirmation slip to pick up on your behalf​:contentReference[oaicite:42]{index=42}.
