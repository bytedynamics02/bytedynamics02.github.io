Our Team

Vaishana Vasuthavan

Given her HR experience spanning over 13 years, Vaishana possesses a deep understanding of the challenges that organizations face in the competitive business world.


Vaishana's expertise in HR and business strategies has made her a sought-after consultant in the industry for IPO, Startups and SMEs. Her ability to understand the unique challenges that each organization faces and tailor effective solutions to address those challenges has earned her the trust of many businesses.


When the pandemic caused significant disruptions in the business world, Vaishana saw an opportunity to use her expertise to help organizations navigate the challenges from the aftermath. Having that in mind, she founded VV Consulting Group, which has since helped several businesses across various industries to assess, identify and address any operational difficulties. Vaishana has also assisted organizations in streamlining their HR processes and developing effective business strategies to stay competitive in the market.
